Taiping, my hometown, is a peaceful and beautiful town filled with nature, history, and amazing food. Known for its cool, rainy weather (seriously, it rains a lot!), stunning Lake Gardens, and historical charm, Taiping is truly one of Malaysia’s most captivating towns.

Growing up here has taught me to enjoy the simple things in life—quiet mornings, delicious local food like cendol, and the calmness that follows the rain. Whether it’s a peaceful stroll at the Lake Gardens, a hike up Bukit Larut (Maxwell Hill) to feel the cool breeze, or just soaking in the serenity of the town, Taiping always feels like home.

Fun fact: Taiping means "everlasting peace," and that’s exactly what it feels like to live here. Sure, the rain might be unpredictable, but I wouldn’t trade this charming little paradise for anything.
