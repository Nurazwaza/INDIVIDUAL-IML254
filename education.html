My Education Journey

Tabika Kemas Tabika Perpaduan SK Taman Palma SMK Kamunting Bakti UiTM Permatang Pauh UiTM Kedah
At the ages of 4 and 5, I began my education journey at Tabika Kemas in my hometown. During this time, my family lived near my grandparents' house, which made it a convenient and cherished start to my schooling. After relocating, I continued my preschool years at Tabika Perpaduan, adapting to a new environment and making new friends. I pursued my primary education from Standard 1 to Standard 6 at Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Palma. While I considered myself an ordinary student academically, I was actively involved in various school activities, which helped shape my character and skills. For my secondary education, I attended Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Kamunting Bakti from Form 1 to Form 5. I had the honor of serving as the Assistant Head Prefect, a role that was both demanding and rewarding. It taught me leadership, responsibility, and teamwork, while also providing countless memorable experiences. Although I couldn’t pursue my dream course at my preferred university, I decided to enroll in the Pra-Diploma program at UiTM Penang. This turned out to be a great stepping stone, as it allowed me to transition smoothly into a diploma program. Thankfully, the program lasted only one semester (about six months), which gave me the foundation I needed to move forward. After completing my Pra-Diploma, I was thrilled to receive an offer to continue my studies in the Diploma in Library Informatics program at UiTM Kedah. Currently, I am in my fourth semester, with just one semester left to complete my diploma. This journey has been a blend of challenges, growth, and achievements, and I look forward to graduating and pursuing my dreams.
